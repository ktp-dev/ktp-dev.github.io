Students

For students interested in attending our company events, they are open to all UM students, unless indicated otherwise. Please sign up for our mailing list to stay up to date with what events we offer. If you would like more information or have questions about rush, please contact us at ktp-board@umich.edu.

Companies

We are always looking to work with companies to put up recruitment events on campus such as tech talks, information sessions, and networking sessions. Such recruitment events help create relationships between students and recruiters in a smaller, more relaxed setting. For companies interested in hosting an event with us or would like more information, please contact us at ktp-board@umich.edu.
