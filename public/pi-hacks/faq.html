Who can participate?

Pi Hacks is open to all current University of Michigan students — any major, any year.

When and where is it?

March 21–22, starting at 10:00 AM at Rackham Auditorium.

How do I register?

Sign up via the Register link in the nav. Sign-ups close March 19th at 11:59 PM EST.

What are the prizes?

$1,500 in prizes distributed across 3 tracks. Details on tracks will be announced at the event.

Do I need a team?

You can register solo or as a team. Team formation help will be available at the start of the event.

What should I bring?

Bring your laptop, charger, and any hardware you plan to use. Food will be provided.

Is there free food?

Yes 👀

What is Red Bull Basement?

Red Bull Basement is a global competition for first-time founders. The 2026 U.S. National Finals are in Ann Arbor on May 8th — Pi Hacks is your launchpad.
