Rush season is kicking off! Join Kappa Theta Pi, the nation's first and largest professional technology fraternity, and become part of a close-knit community of students passionate about technology. Gain access to professional mentorship and connect with our diverse alumni network.
WHO ARE WE?

Founded on January 10, 2012 at the University of Michigan as the first professional technology fraternity in the U.S., Kappa Theta Pi has grown to 25+ active chapters nationwide. Our mission is to foster an inclusive tech community where students develop professionally, build technical expertise, and grow as leaders while forming lifelong connections. Our members and alumni have gone on to make an impact at top companies worldwide.
WHAT DO WE DO?

KTP is dedicated to uniting students across the nation for the love of technology while helping members develop their skills and explore their interests. Our five pillars, Professional Development, Alumni Connections, Social Growth, Technical Advancement, and Academic Support, guide the opportunities we offer. From project teams and study groups to professional development workshops and hackathons or design jams, we create a culture of growth that inspires members to pursue their passions.
HOW DO I JOIN?

If you have an interest in technology, we would love to meet you! We welcome and encourage rushees from every discipline, no matter your major or experience level. The only requirement is that you have at least four semesters left at the University of Michigan, including this semester.
